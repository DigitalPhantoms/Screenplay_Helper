How to Use

Getting Started

  • Format Script: Will format your screenplay in Courier 12 and industry standard margins.
  • Generate Title Page: Use this for the first page to add a title, author, contact info, and date.
  • Intertitle: Add an intertitle such as "Christmas Day" or "A week Ago".
  • Scene Elements: Proper indenting for scene headings, and character actions, names, parentheticals, and dialogue.
  • Add Scene: Add scenes that will appear multiple times for quickly inserting.
  • Add Actor: Add recurring character names for quickly inserting.
  • Transitions: Insert common transition elements like fades and cuts.
  • Script Breakdown: Highlight important elements within the scene for easy tracking by department for your script breakdowns.
  • Script Breakdown Summary: At the end of your script, insert a summary of scenes and their important elements that were highlighted.
